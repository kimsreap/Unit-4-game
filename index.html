Monkey Game

You will be ginven a random number at the start of the game

There are four monkeys below. By clicking on the monkey, you will add a specific amount of point to your total

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each monkey is hidden from you until you click on it

Each time when the game start, the game will change the value of each monkey

Click the monkey to get number closer to the match...
